Water Me

Keep your plants healthy and alive!


Water Me is a web application to help you take care of your plants and keep them healthy and alive. It was born from a pitch idea that I gave for the final project. The idea was to have an app that would help you not only get information about your plant so you can know how to take care of them, but mostly to notify you whenever it needed to be watered. Developed in two weeks by a team of 4 people. developed during the Le Wagon bootcamp as a preparation for the final project. It consists on a app where you can rent things for the day at Le Wagon, be it a sofa, the coffee machine, the ping-pong table or even the teachers. You only need to have enough tokens and schedule the perfect time for you!

It was developed in just two week with the effort of a team of 4 people.Using Ruby on Rails as a back-end language and framework, and the front-end was designed in Figma and created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and aided with Bootstrap, Sass and Stimulus JS libraries.
