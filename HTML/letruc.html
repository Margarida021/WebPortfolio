Le Truc

Now you can rent whatever you want inside Le Wagon!


Le Truc is an Airbnb clone developed during the Le Wagon bootcamp as a preparation for the final project. It consists on a app where you can rent things for the day at Le Wagon, be it a sofa, the coffee machine, the ping-pong table or even the teachers. You only need to have enough tokens and schedule the perfect time for you!

It was developed in just one week with the effort of a team of 4 people. With everyone expertise we brought together what we think was a funny, yet challenging, project. Using Ruby on Rails as a back-end language and framework, and the front-end was designed in Figma and created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and aided with Bootstrap, Sass and Stimulus JS libraries.
